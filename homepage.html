Click for my ko-fi page

When I was a kid, I used to spend hours bouncing on my trampoline and dreaming up awesome game ideas that I could never make a reality. Ten years later, I learned how to code in college and was finally able to put the ideas in my head onto a screen.

In 2016, I started a game development tutorial YouTube channel that had over two thousand subscribers with over 225,000 views. Those days, my videos were twenty minutes, thirty minutes, sometimes more, and I often debugged while on camera.

In 2019, I started a new channel because I wanted to make my videos better. I started the old channel when I didn't really know how to program. Some of my old videos were pretty bad. But I loved the community, and I loved teaching (my mom is a teacher so maybe I got that from her) so I came back to make more YouTube videos.

If you like my work, you might consider donating to my Ko-fi page to fund more tutorials. Those who donate get the chance to make special requests for future game tutorials.

Regardless of what you can give, thanks for being here!
